What are the British Values?

Now take a break from this week's teaching material to learn about British Values. This will be discussed at your tripartite reviews. 

The four core British values that are upheld by the the Apprenticeship team are Democracy, Individual liberty, The Rule of Law, Mutual Respect and Tolerance of those of different faiths and beliefs.
