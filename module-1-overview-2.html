 Interfaces

This week, we will explore the importance of interfaces for making information systems accessible to different types of users across an organisation. We will look at the history of information systems, including the historical categories of information system, before exploring how recent developments have seen a convergence towards information platforms. We will also introduce our featured information platform, ServiceNow, exploring different variants of its interface and learning to navigate both the platform and its wider ecosystem.

  Learning Objective

This week you will cover the following learning objectives:

    • Understand the underlying principles and concepts of data management systems
      • Explain the convergence of disparate data management systems into platforms.
    • Develop information literacy and planning knowledge by comparing and contrasting various options for IT project and portfolio management and how each aligns with different approaches of digital governance
      • Discuss the value of an easy to use and useful data management system.
    • Understand the role of emerging information technologies in producing both strategic opportunities and strategic threats to organisations
      • Begin evaluating a significant data management platform.
    • Research a topic independently to extract and synthesise information from a range of academic and online sources
      • Analyse a significant paper in the field of information systems.
    • Engage in critical thinking
      • Begin evaluating a significant data management platform.
