Workplace Activity


This task will help you to apply the module learning to your workplace.

  • Talk to your colleagues and find out what information systems were implemented in the last decade in your company or organisation.
  • How have different information systems transformed work for you or your colleagues?

 

Apprenticeship Standards

 This week’s workplace activity aligns to the following apprenticeship standards.

Digital and Technology Solutions Professional

 

  • K2: Knows and understands the value of technology investments and how to formulate a business case for a new technology solution, including estimation of both costs and benefits.
  • K9: Knows and understands how to deliver a technology solutions project accurately consistent with business needs.
  • S1: Information Systems: Is able to critically analyse a business domain in order to identify the role of information systems, highlight issues and identify opportunities for improvement through evaluating information systems in relation to their intended purpose and effectiveness.
  • B2: Makes concise, engaging and well-structured verbal presentations, arguments and explanations.
  • B9: Able to conduct effective research, using literature and other media, into IT and business related topics.
  • B11: Flexible attitude.
  • B13: A thorough approach to work.

 

Data Scientist

 

  • S5: Implement data solutions, using relevant software engineering architectures and design patterns. Evaluate Cloud vs. on-premise deployment. Determine the implicit and explicit value of data. Assess value for money and Return on Investment.  Scale a system up/out. Evaluate emerging trends and new approaches. Compare the pros and cons of software applications and techniques.
  • B1: Demonstrates an inquisitive approach: the curiosity to explore new questions, opportunities, data, and techniques; tenacity to improve methods and maximise insights; and relentless creativity in their approach to solutions.
  • B4: Demonstrates consideration of problems in the context of organisation goals.
  • B6: Demonstrates a commitment to keeping up to date with current thinking and maintaining personal development. Including collaborating with the data science community.
