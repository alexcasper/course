Introducing the Simulator

Many of the ServiceNow courses within the ServiceNow Fundamentals Path have a Lesson followed by a related Activity

You will perform these Activities within an instance of ServiceNow known as a Simulator. The Simulator gets introduced as part of course 3.0: Now Platform User Interface Overview

As you progress through the courses, you will perform hands on activities in your Simulator and get them validated before moving onto the next course.

Tips

  • Timings for the courses and especially the Activities are just an estimate. Don’t be concerned or surprised if they take longer.
  • The training material may be for an earlier release of ServiceNow (as the platform is constantly evolving) but the Simulator will always be created for the current release. This is why you might spot slight issues or occasional errors when following along with Activities.
  • DO NOT select to ‘Complete Simulator’ until you have completed all Activities in All courses for the ServiceNow Fundamentals path; this will be at the end of Intensive Foundations 2 course

 

You have accessed your Simulator in an earlier session but watch the video below to learn more about how to access and use the Simulator. This will also show some of the potential issues that may arise and how to handle them.
