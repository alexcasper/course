  Course Overview 

Bootcamp Introduction

Welcome to the NCH - Northeastern University's Service Design and Management bootcamp, delivered in partnership with ServiceNow® . We have designed this course to provide a solid introduction to data management and information systems, to relational database systems and designing operational databases, and to IT Service Management using the ServiceNow® platform.

The academic course, Data Management Systems, looks at the concept of data management, and the diverse range of information systems that have been developed to help us utilise or make sense of data. This course will be covered over the first six modules and will explore how a wide range of enterprises around the world use information and information technology to create better managed, more innovative, and successful organisations. You will also have the opportunity to apply your knowledge of data management systems using industry standard cloud-based technology platform ServiceNow.

In parallel with this two courses, you will be studying material from ServiceNow Fundamentals and IT Service Management Fundamentals. These two courses will allow you to prepare for the Certified Systems Administrator qualification, an entry-level qualification that will validate your ability to maintain an enterprise-grade data management system.

As part of the course, you will complete a multiple choice exam, and complete a report on how a data platform can be used to improve operations in a real or fictional organisation.

Course Description

This first six weeks explore how a wide range of enterprises around the world use information and information technology to create better managed, more innovative, and successful organisations. The 21st century enterprise runs on information, and every part of the business has been transformed by the use of information technology. Today’s business leaders, therefore, must have ready access to timely, accurate, and relevant information to manage effectively in the global economy. You will also have the opportunity to apply your knowledge of data management systems using industry standard cloud-based technology e.g. ServiceNow.

The second four weeks are designed to familiarise you with the underlying principles and concepts behind relational databases. You will learn how to proceed in designing a relational database.  You will then use the reporting language SQL to interrogate relational databases. This will involve creating and accessing data tables, indexing, performing arithmetic operations, loops, arrays, multiple table processing, I/O operations, data type conversions and views. In addition, you will have the opportunity to apply your knowledge of relational databases using an industry standard cloud-based technology e.g. ServiceNow.

The final two weeks you will complete your Capstone Project. This will give you additional practical experience with the ServiceNow platform. You will also complete a number of assignments including a SQL exercise, a database design task and a write up of the IT Service Strategy Project.
