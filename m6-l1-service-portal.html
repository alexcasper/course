 Service Catalog

The Service Catalog is a tool to effectively manage employee requests within an organisation. While it has its origins in managing employee requests for hardware, it can also be used to allow employees to request abstract services, report incidents, or request changes. Requests are typically made through a service portal, a separate interface that makes requests accessible for those without knowledge of the behind-the-scenes data management platform. Those approving the request, and fulfillers - those responsible for ensuring the requested items or services are delivered - are able to view the requests through an interface that helps them keep track of these requests.
