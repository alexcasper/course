Data and Information

Most organisations name capital, people, buildings and equipment as their main resources, and these are the assets that managers spend most time handling.  Another important organisational resource is data and information. It supports the day-to-day operational tasks and activities, enables the routine administration and management of the business, and supports strategic decision making and future planning. Therefore, it is important that data and information are managed and presented in a consistent, accurate, timely and easily understood way.
