Welcome to the Skills Bootcamp in Digital Induction.

This Induction marks the start of your Skills Bootcamp in Digital and provides an opportunity for you to meet with key faculty who will be delivering your training, as well as members of the wider team.

These sessions are designed to give you a full overview of your Programme, guidance on how to use Aptem to evidence your learning, as well as instructions for Canvas, the Virtual Learning Environment. As you navigate through the Induction content, you will become familiar with the functions of Canvas, in preparation for the following course, Data and Service Management.

The Academic Lead will explain in detail all that is required in the upcoming sessions, and of course continue to support you for the duration of your programme. This week you will be required to complete certain tasks that will familiarise you with the systems and processes required throughout the bootcamp.

 Bio of Dr Alex Cline

Alex specialises in Cloud and Data Engineering, and IT Service Management. Alex acts as the academic lead for the Digital Skills Bootcamp programme, and is a ServiceNow Certified System Administrator and Implementation Specialist (ITSM). Alex is also an AWS Academy Authorised Instructor and a Google Cloud Faculty Expert. Alex has an interest in Data Visualisation and is a Tableau Certified Desktop Specialist. Prior to joining NCH, Alex was a lecturer specialising in Systems and Automation at Ada, England’s National College for Digital Skills. Alex undertook research studies at Anglia Ruskin University’s Cultures of the Digital Economy Institute and the Centre for Digital Material Culture at the University of Sussex, working on the preservation and archiving of early digital platforms.
