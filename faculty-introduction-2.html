Bio of Alex Cline

Alex specialises in Cloud and Data Engineering, and is an AWS Academy Authorised Instructor and Faculty Ambassador, and a Google Cloud Faculty Expert. Alex is also a Tableau Certified Desktop Specialist and ServiceNow Certified System Administrator.

Prior to joining Northeastern, Alex was a lecturer specialising in Systems and Automation at Ada, England’s National College for Digital Skills. Alex undertook research studies at Anglia Ruskin University’s Cultures of the Digital Economy Institute, and the Centre for Digital Material Culture at the University of Sussex, working on the preservation and archiving of early software and media art. Alex is an affiliated member of Northeastern University's Lab for Texts, Maps and Networks, and the British Computer Society.

Bio of Dr Nataliya Mogles

Nataliya’s primary interests lie in the areas of design of human-aware intelligent systems and technology-based behaviour change solutions, persuasive technology, safety and sustainability, agent-based modelling and analysis of complex organisations. She received her MSc degree in Cognitive Science and PhD in Artificial Intelligence at VU University Amsterdam. Nataliya co-authored about 30 scientific articles on interdisciplinary technologies and Artificial Intelligence.

 Prior to joining NCH, Nataliya worked as a Business Development Manager within IT training industry, a Research Associate at the University of Bath and the University of Bristol.
