IT Service Management

 Service and Data Management

Data and information management can be used across multiple domains; we might manage data in a hospital, a library, a factory or a store. ServiceNow was originally designed as a IT Service management platform; it served to provide a database of IT resources, of problems and incidents. Learning about ITSM gives us the 'why' of ServiceNow as we discover a practical use case for a data management system. More recently, the platform has expanded to be used in a range of different settings, from university admissions to vaccine rollouts. We find that the principles of IT Service Management, with its structured approaches to problem resolution and resource management, can be applied in a range of different areas. In the example below, a Country Store has adopted the platform to allow it to manage its operations, including its sales, its support to customers and its human relations.
