Introduction to ServiceNow Fundamentals Learning Path

NowLearning is ServiceNow’s online Learning Platform.

ServiceNow Fundamentals Path is the starting point for most roles to begin their ServiceNow journey. The path provides an introduction to the ServiceNow Platform before launching into interactive sessions, use cases and hands on activities that enable you to familiarise yourself with the tool.

In NowLearning, a ‘Learning Path’ is a series of courses that have been grouped together; often ending in a Certification / Exam

The ‘on demand’ format allows you to work at your own pace and retains where you are on the path. This enables you to pause, rewind and replay sections as required.

Within each course, there are a combination of Lessons, Activities and Knowledge Checks. Some courses may only have 2 of the 3 sections but you MUST complete all sections to finish the path.

  • Lesson: involves reading text, watching / interacting with videos and demos
  • Activity: perform practical hands- on exercises in the ServiceNow Simulator; these will need to be validated
  • Knowledge Check: multiple choice questions related to lesson content to check your learning.

Activity

By now, you will have created a ServiceNow NowLearning account.

To access NowLearning and start your ServiceNow journey, please perform the following steps:

  1. Go to: https://nowlearning.service-now.com/lxp (Links to an external site.) and add it to your Bookmarks
  2. Login to your NowLearning account using the email address you provided at registration; you will be emailed a 6-digit passcode each time you Login to NowLearning
  3. Search for and Enrol on ServiceNow Fundamentals Path; there should be no fee for this

 

Activity - Important Action required for your ServiceNow Fundamentals Simulator

Many ServiceNow courses in the ServiceNow Fundamentals Path have a Lesson followed by a related Activity. You will perform these Activities within an instance of ServiceNow known as a Simulator. As you progress through the courses, you will perform hands on activities in your Simulator and get them validated before moving onto the next course.

Simulators are usually available for a couple of weeks. As you will be needing the  Simulator to be available for more than the standard duration, ServiceNow need to know the URL of your simulator so they can extend its duration.

Please follow ALL the steps below:

  1. Within the ServiceNow Fundamentals Path, scroll to the ServiceNow Fundamentals Simulator course; usually the last course in the path
  2. Select the Open Simulator button; shows a list of Simulator tasks (to complete later)
  3. In the page header in the upper-right, select Go to Simulator button; opens a ServiceNow instance in a new browser tab 
  4. Once the Simulator is open, you should see the URL for your Simulator. The example below shows the format of the URL for a Simulator; it will be 'tac' and 5 digits.  

            

  5. Make a note of your Simulator URL and email it along with your name to sarah.marshall@nchlondon.ac.uk.
  6. Behind the scenes, ServiceNow will extend the duration of your Simulator   

It is very important that you perform the above steps as soon as you have started your Simulator
