  Summary & Congratulations

 

In this module, we looked at how Information Technology Service Management as a practice and a methodology is applied in a significant international research organisation. We will also developed your organisational case study, and demonstrated our knowledge of ITSM on ServiceNow through a multiple choice assessment.

Well done!

 

Learning Objectives

You should now be confident with the following learning objectives:

  • K1B Have knowledge and critical understanding of the fundamental principles of ITSM
  • K2B Understand how ITSM can operate in an organisation to improve processes
  • K3B Understand best management practice from a technical and non-technical perspective
  • S1B Put ITSM into practice on a test system
  • S2B Evaluate the best ITSM approach for a given case study and make recommendations
  • T1B Communicate management theories and ideas in writing
  • T2B Problem solve through the lens of management theories
  • T3B Critically evaluate information from a variety of sources

If you are not able to confidently discuss the above, you should revisit the material and perform self-directed research.
