Tips & Badges

 

You should be familiar with NowLearning and ServiceNow and how to proceed with undertaking the ServiceNow Fundamentals courses.

If you require a reminder, please refer to Module 6 of the Data Management Systems course.

Recall that most courses within the ServiceNow Fundamentals Path include short ‘Digitize it’ videos where ServiceNow team members will introduce you to high level content with light banter.

Tips

  • Ensure you watch all the core videos as this is how you get the green circle completed against each course
  • Make sure you scroll to the bottom of all pages as there are often multiple videos / lessons

Badges

  • Once you have completed a course, you will see a message / notification stating you have unlocked a new badge. It will then move you automatically to the next course.3
