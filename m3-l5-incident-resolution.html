Resolution and Closure

Complete the resolution and closure section of ITSM Fundamentals.

Complete the resolve an incident activity. The resolution stage and closure states are neighbours, but should be seen as separate. Effectively, we mark an incident as resolved when we as technicians think the problem is resolved. We need the original caller to confirm that the service is operational, however, before the incident can be closed. Many organisations will choose to automatically close resolved incidents after a certain period of time. Alternatively, an incident admin can close a resolved incident, should they feel that the issue needs no further attention.
