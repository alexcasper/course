Agile and the Cloud

The Cloud makes it easier to provision IT resources according to the needs of the business. Traditionally it would take time to set up resources; it would require networking specialists to be engaged and hardware to be ordered. This pushed organisations towards requiring advanced notice for resources required, putting constraints on teams. With cloud resources, teams can provision their own infrastructure quickly, making it possible for them to change the architecture used for delivery on the fly. They can provision test and development environments and experiment with new architectures.
