Learning Objectives

You should now be able to:

  • Understand the role of emerging information technologies in producing both strategic opportunities and strategic threats to organisations
    • Discuss the value of having accessible information technology services to an organisation.
    • Understand the value of data analysis for process analysis and optimisation.
  • Classify various types of business information systems and how they meet organisational needs by addressing information problems across the value chain
    • Describe the value of service catalogs for supporting information capture and process optimisation.
  • Engage in critical thinking
    • Discuss the importance of low-code programming tools in business contexts.

If you are not able to confidently discuss the above, you should revisit the material and perform self-directed research. If you are still having difficulties, please post a question on the discussion forum.
