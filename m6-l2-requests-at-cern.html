Service Portal at CERN

This Bulletin from November 2011 announces the arrival of the Service Portal at CERN. 

You can also review the live version of the CERN Service Portal. While not every service will be visible, you can see the hierarchy used, and the types of services on offer.

Service Portals originate from ITSM, but now offer a range of different services. Reviewing the bulletin above, what were the departments that initiated the use of the platform, and which departments joined the project later? * IT and GS (General Services) joined the project first. Human Resources and Finance & Procurement adopted it later.

Reviewing the current service portal, we can see that some types of service remain 'out of scope' - they are not covered by the portal. What is an example of an out-of-scope service? *Services related to Engineering and to the Pension fund are covered elsewhere
