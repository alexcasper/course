Service Design

The case study introduction also discusses four dimensions of Service Design. Can you define the following?

  • People The people needed to create and maintain a service, and the way they are organised
  • Process The approaches to service provision and creating value
  • Products The information technology hardware and software used or supported by the service
  • Partners The external vendors or integrations leveraged by the service

You should consider these dimensions when developing and describing your own case studies.
