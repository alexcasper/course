 Formative Assignment

The Formative Assessment asks you to write short answers to the following questions. In total, your answers should be no more than two pages. This assessment is due on the Friday of Week 3 at 23.59pm.

Reflect on your experience with the IT Service Management as an employee within your organisation. What are the technology services that you make use of as an end-user? What are the interfaces that assist you in making use of those services?

Reflect on your experience as a technology practitioner within your organisation. What are the technology services that you are involved in supporting, and how are incidents and problems with these services dealt with? You may wish to refer to the ITIL® concepts of the Service Value Chain and System.

Reflect on the changes you have seen to technology services within your organisation. How are small-scale and major changes handled? How does the process help and hinder your team’s effectiveness?

 Summative Assignment 1

Summative Assignment 1 is a report of 2,500 words, based on your experiential learning as part of the degree apprenticeship. You should structure your report with an introduction, conclusion and properly cited references.

You should answer the following questions:

What are the information technology and information technology-enabled services that are provided by your organisation? What are the offerings that are provided and what types of service requests are made? Who are the consumers of your organisation’s services?

How are service management methodologies used to track incidents and problems within your organisation? How are solutions formulated and shared with people across your organisation?

How are changes regulated within your organisation? What are the technical and non-technical risks associated with these changes, and how does process mitigate these risks?

You may also choose to incorporate elements of your work in the formative assessment. Summative 1 is due on the Friday 2 weeks after the end of the course at 23.59pm.

Question 3

You should refer to change management processes and mechanisms employed within your organisation. You may also wish to discuss conflicts related to change management.

Question 2

You should describe both methodologies or processes and technology platforms. If your organisation uses multiple resources or a bespoke process, you might wish to talk about this, alternatively you may discuss how a more structured approach could be developed.

Question 1

You should refer to the concepts of the service value chain and service value stream when describing service design and delivery. You may wish to talk about services delivered to customers, but you should also consider services that are delivered to others within your organisation.

 Summative Assignment 2

Summative Assignment 2 is a 40 question multiple choice exam, to be held on the Friday of Week 6 at 10am.

This assignment is based on the ServiceNow IT Service Management (ITSM) Fundamentals - On Demand course. Please ensure you have completed the course by Week 6. While the exam is open book, it will not be possible for you to retrieve the information required during the exam period without having a knowledge of how ITSM is implemented on the ServiceNow platform.
