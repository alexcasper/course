Formative Assessment: Preparing Ahead

 

This formative assessment will not count towards your course mark, but it allows you to practice the type of assessment you’ll have to complete to pass the course. It also consolidates learning. 

Download the Formative Assessment Brief for full instructions on the assessment and plan for this assignment.

You will submit this assignment in Week 2.

Formative Assignments are strongly encouraged as an important part of the learning experience to obtain feedback ready for the Summative Assignments.  Please note, draft form will be accepted for Formative Assignments as content is assessed rather than presentation.  Failure to meet deadline submission will be at the course lead’s discretion as to whether they will mark any work submitted after this date.  Request for extensions must be formally applied for through the Extenuating Circumstances Form.
