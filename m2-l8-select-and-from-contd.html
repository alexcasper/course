Think About It!

When mathematical operations are performed containing a mixture of multiplications and additions, for example, multiplication and division are solved first followed by addition and subtraction.

What answer would you get if you type into SQLite3 the following statement: SELECT (2 + 7) * (3 + 5);

Once you are done, click Reveal Answer to view the answer.

Reveal Answer

Reveal Answer

We attain 72 as the answer. The parentheses override that order. Any numbers in brackets are summed first before multiplying the two sums together.

In short, any numbers in brackets are processed first. If you have nested brackets, then the inner most brackets are dealt with first before dealing with any numbers in the outer brackets, and so on and so forth!
