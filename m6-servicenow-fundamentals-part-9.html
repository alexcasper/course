Accessing your ServiceNow Instance

In Module 6 of the Data Management Systems course you were advised that you will have access to a personal ServiceNow Instance for the duration of your apprenticeship degree programme.

In Module 3 of this course (Database Design & Management - I), there was an activity to access your allocated personal ServiceNow Instance . If you did not complete the activity, you will need to do so now in order to continue your ServiceNow Fundamentals Path learning.

The bullet points below are a reminder of what the activity involved:

  • Connecting to your personal ServiceNow Instance (as shown in the video below)
  • Logging on via the admin account using the allocated password (from the email you had received in Week 2).
  • Changing the admin password when prompted (and ensuring the password was noted down for future use).

If you require a reminder on how to connect to your ServiceNow Instance, please watch the video below.

 

Please use your personal ServiceNow Instance for all activities related to Course 15.0 (as these activities do not require validating in the Simulator).

You can also use your personal ServiceNow Instance to follow along with any examples or demonstrations shown in Course 12.0 should you wish to do so. This will also give you the opportunity to become familiar with your ServiceNow Instance.

 

Activity - Using your ServiceNow Instance

Please log in to your allocated ServiceNow Instance via the admin account and using the password you created in Module 3.

  • Take the opportunity to explore the ServiceNow platform in your ServiceNow Instance for about 15 minutes:
    • Click on some of the icons such as Diagnostics, Data Management and System Security, and follow each in turn to see what they display.
    • You can return to the homepage at any time by clicking on Homepage on the left navigation menu.
    • On the left navigation menu, you can click on some of the items such as "Dashboards" and "Service Catalog" or any other item of personal interest.
  • Move on to the next page ("Part 10") to complete Course 12 ("Flow Designer Overview").

If you have any problems accessing your ServiceNow Instance, please email Sarah Marshall (sarah.marshall@nchlondon.ac.uk).
