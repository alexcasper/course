 System Integration

 

 

 

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Understand the role of emerging information technologies in producing both strategic opportunities and strategic threats to organisations
    • Discuss how different types of information systems fulfil the requirements of different management groups.
    • Explain how information systems can allow organisations to collaborate across the business.
  • Classify various types of business information systems and how they meet organisational needs by addressing information problems across the value chain
    • Describe the various roles involved in IT provision and governance.
    • Explain the value chain with reference to particular industries and enterprises.
  • Engage in critical thinking
    • Investigate the way value is defined and measured within organisations.
