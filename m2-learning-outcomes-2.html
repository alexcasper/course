  Summary & Looking Ahead

 

Congratulations you have reached the end of week 2. You have started to put ITSM into practice using the ServiceNow platform.

 

You have learnt about the various forms of request management and other critical information needed to operate IT services. Good work.

 

Next week we will learn about incident management.

 

Learning Objectives

You should now be confident with the following learning objectives:

  • K1B Have knowledge and critical understanding of the fundamental principles of ITSM
  • K2B Understand how ITSM can operate in an organisation to improve processes
  • K3B Understand best management practice from a technical and non-technical perspective
  • S1B Put ITSM into practice on a test system
  • S2B Evaluate the best ITSM approach for a given case study and make recommendations
  • T1B Communicate management theories and ideas in writing
  • T2B Problem solve through the lens of management theories
  • T3B Critically evaluate information from a variety of sources

If you are not able to confidently discuss the above, you should revisit the material and perform self-directed research. If you are still having difficulties, please post a question on the discussion forum.

You should also be prepared to submit your Formative Assignment based on the materials from this week.
