    ServiceNow Fundamentals - Part 2

     

    Activity - Accessing NowLearning and your first course

    1. If you are not logged into NowLearning
      • Use your Bookmark or this link: https://nowlearning.service-now.com/lxp (Links to an external site.) 
      • Login to your account using the email address you provided at registration; you will be emailed a 6-digit passcode each time you Login to NowLearning
    2. Search for and Enrol on 'Get Started with the Now Platform' course (12 modules)

    The main objective of this course is to familiarise yourself with what ServiceNow looks like and introduce some of the essentials before you start the ServiceNow Fundamentals training.

    You will cover this content in detail when you work through the Fundamentals PATH so treat this initial course as a Demonstration of some aspects of ServiceNow and maximise the screen for easy viewing.

    Tips:

    • For videos on NowLearning, you can toggle to Show / Hide Transcript text; to hide it, scroll to the end of the transcript
    • Many videos have the option to increase / decrease the speed of the presenter talking; options appear in the bottom right corner by clicking the 1x button
