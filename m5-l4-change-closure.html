Review and Close

Please complete the Review and Close section of IT Service Management fundamentals.

Complete the close change tasks activity. As with requests, incidents, and problems it is is possible to have change tasks. These are subtasks within the change request representing the work involved in the planning, implementation, testing or review stages. Here, we are going to use change tasks for the implementation and testing stages, but they can also be used to track and allocate the work involved in planning or reviewing a change. Note that after the change is implemented, it will enter the 'Review' state'.

Complete the apply proposed changes to the CI activity. The information about the change to the configuration item, a server having its memory upgraded, was captured in the planning process. If we press 'Apply Proposed Changes' after we have completed the changes, it will also update the item record within our configuration management database.

Complete the close a change request activity. During this exercise, we will need to provide closure information, documenting whether the change was in fact successful. This will allow the request to be closed.
