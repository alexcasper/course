Management Practices

The following guide to the principles was developed by BMC, a technology service management tool provider. While they are an alternative (even a rival) to the ServiceNow platform we are learning about in this course, the ITIL practices are not specific to any particular tool or provider. Rather, they provide a shared set of standard practices. In this reading, BMC distinguishes three practice areas:

  • General Management Practices
  • Service Management Practices
  • Technical Management Practices

While the former comes from business management theory in general (such as supply chain management or process optimisation), and the latter set of practices come from other technology disciplines (such as system development or technology project management), there is a central thread of practices that are originating within the technology service management sector itself.

Rae, B. 2020. ITIL4(R) Guide to Practices. BMC.
