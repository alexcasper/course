Module 2: Principles

Icon Progress Bar/Navigation (built in browser, hidden in app)

This week, we will investigate some core concepts underlying many of the modern information systems we use on a regular basis, including the common tabular display of information seen across spreadsheets, relational databases and a range of information systems. We will look at filters and views, as well as references or lookups from one table to another. We will also explore the concept of the form, understanding how information can be effectively collected from data subjects. Our investigations will be supported with practical experience in the ServiceNow platform.

 

Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Understand the role of emerging information technologies in producing both strategic opportunities and strategic threats to organisations
    • Discuss the significance of information quality for organisations.
  • Classify various types of business information systems and how they meet organisational needs by addressing information problems across the value chain
    • Analyse the value of lists and forms interfaces for processing data.
  • Engage in critical thinking
    • Discuss the concept of the information product and how it can be improved.
