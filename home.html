Data and Service Management

Welcome

Welcome to the NUL - Northeastern University's Service Design and Management bootcamp, delivered in partnership with ServiceNow® . We have designed this course to provide a solid introduction to data management and information systems, to relational database systems and designing operational databases, and to IT Service Management using the ServiceNow® platform.

The academic course, Data Management Systems, looks at the concept of data management, and the diverse range of information systems that have been developed to help us utilise or make sense of data. This course will be covered over the first six modules and will explore how a wide range of enterprises around the world use information and information technology to create better managed, more innovative, and successful organisations. You will also have the opportunity to apply your knowledge of data management systems using industry standard cloud-based technology platform ServiceNow.

In parallel with this course, you will be studying material from ServiceNow Fundamentals and IT Service Management Fundamentals. These two courses will allow you to prepare for the Certified Systems Administrator qualification, an entry-level qualification that will validate your ability to maintain an enterprise-grade data management system.

As part of the course, you will complete a multiple choice exam, and complete a report on how a data platform can be used to improve operations in a real or fictional organisation.
