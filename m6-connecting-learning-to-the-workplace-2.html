Workplace Activity

 

This task will help you to apply the module learning to your workplace.

ServiceNow is an example of a cloud-based platform (PaaS) that offers a variety of services, e.g. IT Service Management.  Research what platform or system is used to automate IT Service Management processes in your organisation.  What standard processes are available for a standard user?

Data Science
