Introduction to Digital Transformation

Overview

A business survey for the Wall Street Journal (WSJ) found that digital transformation (DT) was the number one concern for business leaders in 2019. In the year, US $1.3 trillion was spent on DT, impacting businesses from large corporates to SMEs as everyone attempts to adapt to a digital future. Salesforce defines digital transformation as the process of using digital technologies to create new — or modify existing — business processes, culture, and customer experiences to meet changing business and market requirements. This reimagining of business in the digital age is digital transformation.

Digital transformation therefore goes beyond digitisation: the move from analogue to digital meant the storing and sharing of information became much more efficient, but the ways in which businesses operated remained fundamentally the same. Digital transformation encapsulates an entire business transformation that changes business culture, how businesses operate and how they deliver value to customers. This includes changing existing business models, as well as creating entirely new ones.
