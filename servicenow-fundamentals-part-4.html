Introduction to ServiceNow Fundamentals eBook

ServiceNow courses are often available as ‘on-demand’ where learners control when they complete the modules and virtual instructor led courses (VILT). Our approach is to deliver most ServiceNow courses ‘on-demand’ but through this programme you also have access to the VILT ServiceNow Fundamentals eBook; an online version of the course content.

The on-demand and VILT SN Fundamentals courses cover very similar content but the flow is slightly different. Note, you will be covering the on-demand courses in a slightly different order to ensure the most relevant content is covered within Data Management Systems and Database Design and Management courses. Intensive Foundations II will cover the remaining courses to ensure you complete the SN Fundamentals Path by the end of the 3 courses.

Note: The Lab exercises in the eBook mentioned above are different to the on-demand course and we will not be covering the Capstone project as part of your learning. However, once you have your own ServiceNow NCH personal instance (assigned to you during Database Design and Management I course), you can explore these areas in your own time if you choose.

This eBook can be used to support your on-demand learning and to refer back to content that is displayed online as you progress through the courses.

How to access the eBook

You will have been sent an email to your NCH email address from noreply@gilmore.ca

  • The email contains:
    • Your eBook license code
    • Steps required to register with eVantage (VitalSource Bookshelf)
    • How to redeem your license code
    • Instructions on How to view your eBook
  • You need to follow all steps to access the eBook

The most efficient way to use the eBook is to use the Table of Contents to search for the relevant section. Note that ServiceNow content covered in Data Management Systems will appear in Module 1 or Module 2 of the eBook
