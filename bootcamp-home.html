Welcome

Welcome to the Skills Bootcamp virtual induction. This will be an introduction of what to expect as you move through your programme.

  Welcome

Day 1: Introduction, Study Tools and ServiceNow

Day 1 gives you an opportunity to meet members of the team, be introduced to methods for effective academic study, introduce you to Careers support, as well as have an introduction to the ServiceNow learning platform NowLearning. We will also introduce you to the ServiceNow platform, the career advising process and the underlying values of responsible data and service management.

  Go to Day 1

Day 2: Academic Study, Databases and Incident Management

On day 2 you will learn how to use the virtual learning environment and learner tracker and we will cover information on safeguarding and ensuring your wellbeing. You will also learn about Service Design.

  Go to Day 2

Day 3: The ServiceNow Platform

We will begin day 3 with a session on The ServiceNow platform. There will also be an open-door session where you will have an opportunity to ask any questions you may have at this stage to academic and support staff.

  Go to Day 3

Day 4: Platform development and technology service management

Day 4 provides the opportunity to learn about Technology Service Management and Platform Development.  We will also hold a social quiz, which is an opportunity for you to get to know some of your fellow learners in a less formal environment.

  Go to Day 4

Day 5: Digital Innovation

On day 5, you will have a plenary on preparing for next week and an opportunity to ask questions on what is coming up next. There will be an opportunity for you to evaluate the Bootcamp so far. There is also a lesson on Digital Innovation.

  Go to Day 5
