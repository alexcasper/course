Introduction to ServiceNow's online learning platform

 

ServiceNow is a cloud-based workflow automation platform that enables enterprise organisations to improve operational efficiencies by streamlining and automating routine work tasks.

NowLearning is ServiceNow’s Learning Platform where you will access ServiceNow training courses as part of your degree.

 

Activity - Register for a NowLearning account

To gain ‘free’ access to ServiceNow training, please register for a NowLearning account by following the steps below:

1) Identify which email address to use

When registering, please use the relevant email address below depending on your situation

  • If you already have a ServiceNow NowLearning account, please notify sarah.marshall@nchlondon.ac.uk of the email address you used to register
  • If you work for a ServiceNow partner, register using your work email address as your organisation will earn 'points’ for each ServiceNow course you complete
  • If you don’t currently have a NowLearning account, please use your NCH email address to register

 

2) Watch the video below

This video shows how to Create a ServiceNow NowLearning account

 

   

 

3) Watch Part 2 video below

This video shows how to complete account setup and walks you through how to familiarise yourself with the Now Learning platform.

 

 

 

4) Go to https://nowlearning.service-now.com/lxp and register for a NowLearning account

 

Activity - After you have registered for a NowLearning account

Email sarah.marshall@nchlondon.ac.uk stating the email address you used to setup your NowLearning account.

*** This needs to be done ASAP to ensure that ServiceNow upgrade your account and give you access to the ServiceNow Fundamentals Path *** and other training courses.

Note: You will NOT be able to Enrol on the ServiceNow Fundamentals Path until you have completed the steps above and you have been notified that ServiceNow have upgraded your NowLearning account.
