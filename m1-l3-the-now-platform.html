 The Now Platform

As part of this course, we will be looking at the ServiceNow platform in detail. ServiceNow is a cloud-based workflow automation platform that enables enterprise organisations to improve operational efficiencies by streamlining and automating routine work tasks. The Now Platform has its origins in IT Service Management, but has grown to encompass a range of different functions. The outer ring features different products, targeted at different areas of a business. On the inner ring are the various applications. In the centre are the different features that have been developed; these work across applications to extend their functionality. This diagram is from 2019 - as such, it is already slightly out of date - but this visualisation shows comprehensively what we mean by platform.

 

 

An organisation might not see the need to use every product or application; similarly, a team might find that only a few applications are useful for their particular business case. They might choose also to develop their own custom features or functionality on top of the platform to meet their specific business case. This is where development becomes important; mindful of customers' desire to meet their specific requirements, ServiceNow allows access to underlying data through an application programming interface (API). The platform also features a scripting language based on JavaScript, called GlideScript, as well as low/no code development solutions such as flow designer. For this course, however, we will be only looking at system administration; that is to say, the management and maintenance of unmodified systems.

Now Learning Platform

NowLearning is ServiceNow’s online Learning Platform.

In NowLearning, a ‘Learning Path’ is a series of courses grouped together; often ending in a Certification / Exam.

As part of this class, you will complete ServiceNow Administration Fundamentals; an online self-paced path that introduces you to the ServiceNow platform before launching into interactive sessions, use cases and hands on activities that enable you to familiarise yourself with the tool.

Questions on the platform are the basis for Summative Assessment 2, worth 30% of the grade for those taking the course for credit. The material will also help prepare you for the ServiceNow Certified Systems Administrator (CSA) certification.
