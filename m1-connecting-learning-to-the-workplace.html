Workplace Activity

 

This task will help you to apply the module learning to your workplace and can be uploaded to Aptem as part of your portfolio of evidence to satisfy the apprenticeship standard, as such, you are strongly encouraged to complete it.

  1. Identify different data sources in your company. How does data occur here, and how is it processed?
  2. Can you identify potential issues that could affect the data quality?
  3. Discuss with your line manager how bad data can impact business decisions.

Research an example from your sector/industry to illustrate the impact of bad data.
