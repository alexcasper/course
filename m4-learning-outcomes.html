Summary & Looking Ahead

 

In this module, we focused on data management and fundamental visualisations/decision-making methods by using Tableau. We used Tableau, that is a popular data management platform for exploring, understanding and taking decisions based on your data. We learned how to carry out data discovery methods and techniques for analysis and storytelling.

Module 4 covered the following subjects:

  • We explored data analysis pipelines with Tableau by following a tutorial.
  • We created data analysis solutions, and we learned how to create dashboards and visualisations in Tableau
  • We developed data decision-making strategies with Tableau

Learning Objectives

This week you covered the following learning objectives:

  • Understand the role of emerging information technologies in producing both strategic opportunities and strategic threats to organisations
    • Describe the impact of information systems on organisations.
  • Classify various types of business information systems and how they meet organisational needs by addressing information problems across the value chain
    • Deploy a specific business intelligence system, producing a dashboard to support organisational needs.
  • Apply analytic frameworks to assess the strategic value of information systems
    • Explain how IT can be aligned with the business and use information systems.
    • Decide how and when to apply Business Intelligence tools.
  • Research a topic independently to extract and synthesise information from a range of academic and online source
  • Relate knowledge of theory to practice
  • Engage in critical thinking
